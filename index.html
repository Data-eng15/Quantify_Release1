Frequently Asked Questions

How does the LLM-based model solve mathematical problems from images?

Our LLM-based model analyzes images containing mathematical problems by utilizing advanced image recognition techniques and natural language processing. It accurately detects the equations in the image and provides immediate, step-by-step solutions, making it perfect for solving complex mathematical problems with precision.

Can I draw mathematical equations and get instant answers with the handwritten recognition feature?

Yes! With our handwritten equation recognition feature, similar to Apple’s Notes calculator, you can draw equations on a virtual canvas. The system instantly recognizes the handwritten equations and provides accurate solutions in real-time, giving you an intuitive way to solve math problems.

What kind of user gestures are supported by the OpenCV-based object detection model?

Our OpenCV-based model supports intuitive hand gestures for hands-free problem-solving. You can use gestures like pointing, swiping, or specific finger combinations to input equations, clear the canvas, or trigger the AI for solving equations. This approach offers a seamless and interactive way to perform calculations.

Do I need any special hardware or devices to use these models?

No special hardware is required! Our system uses your device's camera to detect gestures and analyze handwritten equations. Whether you upload images or draw on the screen, everything is processed in real-time using the device you already have, without the need for extra tools.

Can the models handle both simple and complex mathematical expressions?

Yes, all three models are designed to handle a wide range of mathematical expressions, from basic arithmetic to more advanced topics like calculus, matrix operations, and algebra. Whether you need quick answers for simple problems or detailed solutions for complex equations, the system has you covered
